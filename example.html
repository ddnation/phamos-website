The Science

What is ceramic coating?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that bonds to your paint, creating a durable protective layer. It helps resist UV damage, grime, and chemical contaminants — and makes washing easier.

What you’ll notice

Deeper gloss, easier washes, and paint that stays cleaner longer.

Hydrophobic Water and dirt bead off so washes take less time.
UV resistance Helps reduce fading and oxidation from sun exposure.
Mirror gloss Enhances clarity for a “wet look” finish.
Protection Helps resist light swirls and common contaminants.
